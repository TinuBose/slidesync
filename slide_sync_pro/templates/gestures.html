Slide Sync
Next Page

Use your pinky finger on your right hand to synchronize the next page of the presentation.

Previous Page

Use your thumb finger on your right hand to synchronize the previous page of the presentation.
Zoom In

Use your four finger gesture to zoom in the current slide.

Zoom Out

Use your five finger gesture to zoom out the current slide.

Marker Tool

Use your two finger gesture to introduce and move the pen tool.

Marker

Use your index finger to mark on the current slide.

Undo

Use your three finger gesture to undo the markings.
